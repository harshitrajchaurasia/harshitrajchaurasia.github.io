PII Shield

Smart Ticket Classifier

                        

                            
problem160K+ tickets and change requests a month were triaged and routed by hand - slow, inconsistent, and a constant drain on senior engineers.

                            
approachA BERT bi-encoder retrieves candidate queues, a cross-encoder re-ranks them - tuned for precision over recall so a misroute stays rarer than a near-miss.

                            
result97% accuracy, 85% faster resolution, catalog selection fully automated - granted a TCS copyright.
